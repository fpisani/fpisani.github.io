About

I am currently persuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science at the LMCAD lab of the Institute of Computing, University of Campinas (UNICAMP). My advisor is Prof. Edson Borin.

My research interests include high-performance computing, parallel computing, parallel programming models and frameworks, constrained devices, the Internet of Things, and fog computing.

I received a B.Sc. degree with honors in Computer Science from UNICAMP in 2014.
