About

I received a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Campinas (UNICAMP) in 2019, for which I was advised by Prof. Edson Borin. My dissertation is related to the topics of the Internet of Things (IoT), resource-constrained devices, and fog computing. I received a B.Sc. degree with honors in Computer Science from UNICAMP in 2014.

My current research interests include high-performance computing, parallel computing, fog computing, constrained devices, the Internet of Things, Computer Science education, computational thinking, and the use of technology in education.
